FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Auren is the first collar designed specifically for AI-generated storytelling. Unlike GPS trackers or action cameras, Auren captures your pet's day from their first-person perspective and automatically creates Vlogs, comics, and diary entries — so you don't just see where they went, you experience their day as they lived it.
Auren uses smart intermittent recording — it captures short clips when it detects interesting moments, not continuous footage. You have full control through the app: adjust sensitivity, set quiet hours, or turn off indoor recording entirely. Privacy is designed in from day one.
All content is stored in your personal encrypted cloud vault. Only you (and anyone you explicitly share with) can access your pet's stories. We never train our AI on your pet's data without explicit consent.
Auren weighs just 32g — about as much as a standard ID tag. The soft silicone strap adjusts to fit necks from 8" to 24". Most pets forget they're wearing it within a few hours. We also include a gradual introduction guide in the app.
Auren's AI has been trained on over 47,000 real pet moments. It recognizes 200+ behaviors across dogs and cats, and it gets smarter about YOUR specific pet the more they wear it. Beta testers report 89% accuracy at identifying meaningful moments after just one week.
